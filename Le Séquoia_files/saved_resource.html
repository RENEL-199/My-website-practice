About

Chef RPG is a single-player cooking game RPG made with pixel graphics. The player takes the role of a young chef from Billboard City who has been given the task by their mentor of reviving Le Squoia, a once famous restaurant set in the small seaside town of White Ash Harbor. Hunt, harvest, and shop for ingredients as you run your restaurant. Along the way, befriend fascinating locals and help revive the town as a tourist hotspot. Craft your culinary adventure in this open-ended RPG, where you can choose how to grow and manage your restaurants.
